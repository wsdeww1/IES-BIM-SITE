About IES

Our Story

A specialized engineering services company delivering precision-driven BIM and design solutions across the Middle East.

Built on Engineering Precision

Integrated Engineering Systems (IES) was founded with a clear mission: to deliver high-quality, BIM-based digital modeling solutions that meet the demanding standards of modern construction. Headquartered in Riyadh with a branch office in Cairo, we serve clients across the Middle East and international markets.

Our team of 25+ engineering professionals brings together deep expertise across Architectural, Structural, and MEP disciplines. With members holding 15+ years of industry experience, we have built a track record of 90% on-time submission and successful delivery on mega-projects including high-rise buildings, hospitals, commercial developments, and stadiums.

We believe in reducing project risks, enhancing coordination, and achieving successful outcomes through accurate, coordinated, and detail-driven engineering services.
